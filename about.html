About Me

I am an aspiring supply chain and operations professional with over five years of experience in fast-paced culinary environments, where I developed strong practical skills in inventory management, stock control, supplier coordination, quality assurance, and cost control.

Working in kitchen operations taught me how to manage time-sensitive processes, reduce waste, maintain standards, and make decisions under pressure — all of which are directly relevant to supply chain and operations roles.

To build on this hands-on experience, I completed a B.Sc. in Supply Chain Management, along with the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate and the Unilever Supply Chain Data Analyst Professional Certificate. Through this combination of operational experience and formal training, I have developed a strong interest in procurement, logistics, demand planning, and data-driven decision-making.

I am now seeking opportunities in supply chain, operations, procurement, or analytics where I can apply my problem-solving mindset, analytical skills, and real-world experience to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and support smarter business decisions.

My Transition Story

My professional journey began in culinary operations, where I gained practical experience managing stock, coordinating supplies, controlling costs, and maintaining consistent quality in high-pressure environments.

Over time, I became increasingly interested in the systems behind operations — how procurement, logistics, planning, and data all work together to support performance and customer satisfaction. That interest led me to formally study Supply Chain Management and data analytics.

Today, I am building on both worlds: practical operational experience from the workplace and technical supply chain knowledge from my academic and professional training. My goal is to contribute to organizations that value efficiency, continuous improvement, and data-informed decision-making.
