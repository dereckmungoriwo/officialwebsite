Professional Profile

I am a supply chain and operations-focused professional with more than five years of experience in fast-paced operational environments, where inventory accuracy, supplier coordination, quality control, and cost discipline were part of everyday execution.

My experience has developed a strong practical foundation in stock control, process discipline, time-sensitive coordination, and working effectively under pressure. These are the same principles that support strong supply chain and operational performance across industries.

To strengthen that operational background, I completed a B.Sc. in Supply Chain Management and expanded my analytical capabilities through the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate and the Unilever Supply Chain Data Analyst Professional Certificate.

Today, I bring a combination of practical operational experience, formal supply chain knowledge, and data-driven thinking. I am focused on contributing in roles related to supply chain, procurement, operations, logistics, or analytics, where efficiency, structure, and informed decision-making matter.

Career Direction

My professional journey began in culinary operations, where I worked in environments that demanded speed, consistency, resource awareness, and operational control. Managing stock, coordinating supplies, controlling waste, and maintaining quality standards gave me direct exposure to the realities of day-to-day operations.

Over time, my interest grew beyond execution alone. I became increasingly interested in the systems behind operational performance: how procurement decisions affect availability, how planning affects efficiency, how data reveals patterns, and how structured processes improve outcomes.

That interest led me to formally study Supply Chain Management and develop practical analytical skills that support reporting, insight generation, and better decision-making. The result is a professional profile that combines hands-on experience with a broader understanding of how operations, supply chain, and analytics work together.

My goal is to contribute to organisations that value discipline, continuous improvement, and data-informed performance, while continuing to grow in roles where operational understanding and analytical thinking create measurable value.
